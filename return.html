Returns & Exchanges

We strive for your complete satisfaction. Here are our policies regarding returns and exchanges:

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be in original condition with tags attached.
  • Final sale items are not eligible for returns.

Exchange Policy

  • You may exchange an item for a different size or color within 30 days.
  • Contact customer support to initiate an exchange.
  • Shipping costs for returns/exchanges may apply, unless faulty.

How to Return or Exchange

Please contact our support team at digitalcreatorshivam@gmail.com to start the process. We will guide you through shipping instructions.
